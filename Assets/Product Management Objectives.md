### Usability


The Usability Engineering process shall strive to provide answer to:
* What is the probability of a caregiver misinterpreting the urgency of a patient condition when there is definitive and accurate information in the system indicating need of attention or care?
* What is the probability of a caregiver configuring a treatment plan (scheduling, warning counts) that is not optimal for the collection of relevant (Completion of input data to AMI or Prediction of exacerbations) data on the patient condition?
* What is the probability of a patient commencing negative behaviour in relation to their condition because of their interaction with the system?

The objective of product management with tregards to Usability in Previct Alcohol is to:
* Provide evicence that the [[Users]] can interact with the user interface of the device to achieve the clinical benefits intended with minimal risk of use error if use error may decrease the clinical performance or the safety of the patient. 
* Identify and evaluate possible hazardous user interactions with the user interface prior to placing new designs of the user interface on the market.
* Implement safe design to minimize the risks related to the users interaction with the user interface.
* Include user interface interaction related to the medical functionality of the device in post market surveillance.
