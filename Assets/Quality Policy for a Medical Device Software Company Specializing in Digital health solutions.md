## Introduction

This Quality Policy delineates the commitment of Platform24 to the development and maintenance of superior medical device software solutions for triage and remote patient monitoring. Our dedication to excellence in software design, development, and implementation is aimed at improving patient outcomes, enhancing healthcare delivery, and ensuring compliance with international regulatory standards and best practices within the medical device industry.

## Commitment to Regulatory Compliance and Standards

### Regulatory Compliance

The Company is steadfast in its commitment to comply with all applicable international regulatory requirements and standards that govern the design, development, testing, and deployment of medical device software. This includes adherence to the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) 13485 for medical devices and the software-specific ISO 62304 for medical device software lifecycle processes. Additionally, compliance with the European Union’s Medical Device Regulation (MDR) is rigorously maintained to ensure our products meet the highest regulatory standards.

### Continuous Improvement

Dedication to the principle of continuous improvement is a cornerstone of our quality management system (QMS). Through regular process review and optimization, incorporating stakeholder feedback, and embracing advancements in technology and development practices, we aim to enhance product quality, safety, and efficacy. Our continuous improvement initiatives also focus on streamlining development processes and enhancing operational efficiency.

## Focus on Patient Safety and Product Effectiveness

### Patient Safety

Ensuring the safety of patients utilizing our digital solutions is of utmost importance. The Company employs comprehensive risk management practices, in line with ISO 14971, throughout the software lifecycle. This involves thorough risk analysis, management, and mitigation strategies to prevent or minimize risks to patient health.

### Product Effectiveness

Our products are engineered to provide accurate, reliable, and timely information to healthcare professionals for effective patient triage and monitoring. By leveraging advanced technologies and methodologies, we strive to improve the functionality and usability of our software, ensuring it meets clinical needs and contributes to enhanced healthcare outcomes.

## Customer Satisfaction and Stakeholder Engagement

### Customer Satisfaction

Recognizing the critical role of customer satisfaction in the medical device industry, the Company is committed to understanding and surpassing the expectations of our users, which include healthcare professionals and patients. We actively seek customer feedback as a valuable resource for continuous product and service enhancement.

### Stakeholder Engagement

Engaging with industry partners, healthcare organizations, and other stakeholders is vital for aligning our products with current and future healthcare requirements. The Company promotes open communication and collaboration with stakeholders to remain informed about emerging trends, regulatory updates, and technological advancements. This ensures that our software solutions remain relevant, compliant, and capable of addressing the dynamic challenges of healthcare.
