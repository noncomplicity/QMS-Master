# Methodology

The evaluation was based on a risk-based approach, taking into account potential cybersecurity threats and vulnerabilities. A combination of automated tools, manual testing, and third-party assessments were used.

# Risk Assessment

Key risks identified include:

-   Unauthorized access to patient data.
-   Manipulation of device operation by unauthorized individuals.
-   Data corruption due to malware or malicious attack.
-   Interruption of device functionality.

# Mitigation Measures

## Authentication and Authorization

-   Multi-factor authentication for all users.
-   Role-based access controls.

## Data Encryption

-   AES-256 encryption for data at rest.
-   TLS 1.3 for data in transit.

## Regular Updates and Patches

-   Scheduled software updates.
-   Immediate patches for identified vulnerabilities.

## Network Security

-   Firewalls and intrusion detection systems in place.
-   Regular monitoring and logging.

## Backup and Recovery

-   Regular backups of patient data.
-   Disaster recovery plan tested bi-annually.
