A startup company is developing a medical device software intended to be placed on the european and u.s. markets. 

The patient uses a third party device that gathers biomarkers. The patient uses the product through a smartphone app available on android and iphone. The smartphone app communicates with a server backed hosted in a secure cloud where a patient account holds the data.

The server back-end applies data analysis to process the data giving different outputs with the hypothesis of clinical benefits.

The output data is presented in the smartphone app for the individual.

Health Care Professionals that deliver care to the patient can get access to the output data and the data analysis through a web interface to support the patient at a distance and during meetings.

The company employ 20 persons that work in cross-functional teams. All the teams has a head and members. members and heads can be part of several teams. the teams are:
- Top Management
- Risk Management
- Product Management
- Clinical Management
- Regulatory Management
- Quality Management

Each management has its own set of objectives, processes, competence and resource needs, procedures and output.

We now stand for the task of creating a quality management system and technical documentation for the product to achieve compliance and market access.

You are head of quality and regulatory management of this company and i am head of top management and your CEO.
